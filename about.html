Dr. Anuprita Gandhi Bhatt

Dr. Anuprita Gandhi Bhatt is a leading Phacoemulsification surgeon and a Medical Retina Specialist. Anuprita Gandhi Bhatt completed her MBBS from Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik. She also completed her degree in Ophthalmology from Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital, Pune.

She further pursued training in Community Cataract and Advanced Cataract Surgery from the world famous, Sankar Nethralaya, Chennai. She also completed her Glaucoma training from the renowned Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai. She has done her Medical Retina fellowship at the H. V. Desai Eye hospital, Pune.

Dr. Anuprita Gandhi Bhatt is a fellow of international Council of Ophthalmology, United Kingdom. Her main expertise lies in managing all types of Cataract cases along with the complicated Cataract cases and diagnosis and management of all type of medical retina conditions like diabetic retinopathy, ARNO etc.

She's been part of various trials as primary investigator and sub-investigator. She is also acting as a medical lead in the developing drugs one of the prominent pharmaceutical company. Her medical surgical video of complicated cataract Kyan has received an award in ophthalmic conference.

30,000 + Phacoemulsification cases and more than 40,000 small inarion cataract cases.


She is a designated trainer for Phacoemulsification cataract surgery, SICS surgery cases in ophthalmology and medical retina wherein she has trained numerous national and international students for the same.

Beyond all this, she is compassionate, courteous and a humble person striving towards better quality of life for the patients.
